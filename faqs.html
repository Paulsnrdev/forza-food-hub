Orders & Delivery

Browse the menu on our Full Menu page, select your items and add them to your cart, then click "Checkout" in the cart sidebar. You'll receive a confirmation notification once your order is accepted.
Most orders are ready within 10–20 minutes during normal hours. During peak lunch hours (12 PM – 2 PM), it may take up to 30 minutes. Meal plan holders with Priority access get served first!
You can cancel or modify your order within 5 minutes of placing it. After preparation begins, cancellations are not possible. Visit Order History or contact us at +234 703 456 7890.
Currently, orders are pick-up only from the FORZA FOOD HUB counter. We're working on in-campus delivery for premium meal plan holders. Stay tuned for updates!
You'll be notified immediately and given the option to select a replacement item of equal or lesser value, or receive a full refund to your FORZA wallet.

Meal Plans

We offer three plans: Basic (₦5,000/week) — 5 lunches + daily snack; Student (₦8,500/week) — 5 lunches + 5 breakfasts + 2 daily snacks + priority ordering; Premium (₦12,000/week) — unlimited meals, all beverages, and VIP access. See Meal Plans for full details.
Visit our Meal Plan Signup page, choose your preferred plan, fill in your student details, and complete payment. Your plan activates the next school day.
Yes! You can pause your plan for up to 3 days per week (e.g. during exams or illness). Unused days roll over to the next week. Contact us at least 24 hours in advance to pause.
Yes! You can change your plan at the start of any new week. Upgrades take effect the next day; downgrades apply from the following Monday. Log in to My Account to manage your plan.
Our standard plans cover Monday to Friday. However, the cafeteria is open on weekends (Saturday 10 AM – 6 PM, Sunday 12 PM – 6 PM) for à la carte ordering.

Payment & Pricing

We accept cash at the counter, bank transfers (Paystack), USSD, and FORZA Wallet top-ups. Card payments (Visa/Mastercard) are also available via our POS terminal.
Yes! A 10% student discount is automatically applied to all orders of ₦1,000 and above. Meal plan subscribers get additional savings of up to 30% compared to regular prices.
Load your FORZA Wallet at any time via bank transfer or at the counter. Use your wallet balance to pay instantly without cash.
Refunds are processed for cancelled orders within 48 hours. Refunds go back to your FORZA Wallet or original payment method. For disputes, contact us at forza@schoolcafe.edu.ng.
